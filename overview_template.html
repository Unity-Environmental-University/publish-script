Honor Code, Code of Conduct, and Tech for Success

By participating in this course, you agree:

  • To adhere to the Unity DE Honor Code and Code of Code of Conduct, found in the Unity DE Student HandbookLinks to an external site. at all times. 

    Violations are subject to formal academic review and may result in grade penalties (e.g., no credit for plagiarized work), administrative course withdrawal, or dismissal from Unity Environmental University.

  • That you have access to the equipment, skills and time necessary for completing Unity DE courses, as stated on the Technology for SuccessLinks to an external site. webpage.  

    It is your responsibility to ensure that you have the minimum requirements necessary to fully access and receive support from our staff and faculty. Without the Required Technology Skills for Coursework, you may not be able to access all course materials and/or complete certain assignments, and you will still be financially responsible for the course.
